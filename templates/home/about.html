About Us

Welcome to StockFlow—Where Your Stock Finds Its Flow. We believe that inventory management shouldn’t be a chaotic, manual struggle. Founded by former warehouse managers and software engineers, StockFlow was born out of frustration with expensive, complex systems that didn’t actually solve the day-to-day problems of tracking stock. We saw too many businesses losing sales due to overstocking, stockouts, and inaccurate data. Our mission is to provide intuitive, real-time inventory tracking that brings clarity to your operations. Whether you are managing inventory across multiple warehouses, selling on various e-commerce channels, or tracking raw materials, StockFlow bridges the gap between your physical inventory and your digital records. We don't just sell software; we provide the operational agility you need to grow your business without the overhead.

Our Features

  • Add / Edit / Delete products
  • SKU / Product code and Category & Sub-category of products
  • Unit price and cost price of products

  • Current stock quantity

  • Automatic stock update after transactions

  • Customer records
  • Supplier records
  • Purchase history & Quantity sold & date

  • Monthly sales trend
  • Category-wise stock distribution
